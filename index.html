Decrypt

Your mission is to decrypt a message provided in 3 words and then rearrange the decrypted words to create a meaningful sentence. If you succeed, a crucial clue related to the case will be revealed.

You were provided with a message encrypted word by word. Each word is represented by a series of characters that need to be decrypted. Decrypt each word using the decryption panel. Pay close attention to each word's context and meaning as you decrypt them.

Once you've decrypted all the words, arrange them in the correct order to create a coherent sentence in the notes sheet. Think critically and use your deductive reasoning to ensure the sentence makes sense in the context of the case.

Enter your sentence into the input field provided. Click the "Submit" button to submit your answer for verification.

If your submitted sentence is correct, a crucial clue related to the case will be revealed. Successfully decrypting the message and forming the correct sentence will bring you one step closer to solving the case. Take note of the revealed clue, as it may hold the key to unraveling the mystery and bringing the culprit to justice.

Good luck, detective. Gotham City is counting on you.
